Feature Gallery

Homepage

The homepage showcases a curated list of AI solutions with filters by category, use case, and popularity. It's designed to help users quickly discover tools that fit their business needs.

Community Page

A built-in community hub where users can ask questions, share insights, and review AI tools. This feature encourages peer-to-peer support and crowdsourced feedback on solutions.

Payment & Invoicing

Secure and seamless checkout flow powered by Stripe Connect. Users can purchase tools, receive downloadable invoices, and sellers can manage payouts from their dashboard.

Resource Hub

An AI-assisted directory of grants, government programs, and business support schemes. Companies can discover relevant opportunities, and the AI engine recommends the most suitable ones based on their business profile.

Through extensive market research and analysis of the AI ecosystem, we identified critical pain points that were hindering the growth of both businesses and AI startups:

  • For Startups: Lacked visibility and reach to connect with potential customers
  • Market Gap: No centralized platform existed to bridge the gap between AI solution providers and businesses
  • Trust Issues: Businesses hesitated to adopt AI solutions due to lack of credible reviews

This research phase involved analyzing competitor platforms, studying industry reports, and conducting initial surveys with both business owners and AI startup founders to validate our assumptions.

  • Community Features: Developed the discussion forums, review system, and user interaction mechanisms
Team Collaboration:
  • Frontend Development: Worked closely with teammates on the user interface and experience design
  • Backend Architecture: Collaborated on database design and API development using Next.js and Prisma

My Role

  • Designed and implemented all gameplay mechanics from scratch.
  • Developed the battle logic, card collection, and trading systems.

Feature Gallery

Battle System

Designed and coded the core battle mechanics, including move selection, health management, and turn-based combat with engaging console animations.

Card Pack Collecting

Implemented a card pack opening system with randomization, collection tracking, and rarity-based rewards to create an engaging collecting experience.

Work Process

1
Concept Development & Learning Objectives

This project began as a personal exploration into C# programming and game development fundamentals, driven by my passion for both Pokémon and console-based applications:

Technical Goals:
  • C# Mastery: Develop proficiency in object-oriented programming concepts and C# syntax
  • Game Logic: Understand state management, turn-based systems, and randomization algorithms
Design Inspiration:
  • Pokémon Mechanics: Recreate core battle systems, type effectiveness, and card collecting
  • Nostalgic Experience: Capture the essence of classic Pokémon games in a modern console format

The concept aimed to blend learning objectives with entertainment value, creating a project that would be both technically challenging and personally rewarding to develop.

2
Game Logic Design & Architecture

The core phase involved designing and implementing the fundamental game mechanics that would drive the entire user experience:

Battle System Architecture:
  • Type Effectiveness: Created a comprehensive type chart system affecting battle outcomes
  • Health Management: Developed HP tracking, status effects, and battle conclusion logic
Collection & Progression:
  • Card Statistics: Designed balanced stats, rarity systems, and card progression mechanics
  • Pack Opening: Implemented randomization algorithms for fair and exciting card distribution
Design Challenge

Creating balanced and engaging gameplay mechanics while maintaining the authenticity of Pokémon battle systems in a simplified console environment.

Strategic Solution

Focused on core mechanics that translate well to text-based interfaces: turn-based combat with clear visual feedback, simplified but authentic type effectiveness, and streamlined card management systems.

3
UI Development with Spectre.Console

Leveraged the Spectre.Console library to transform the standard console experience into an engaging, colorful, and interactive gaming interface that rivaled traditional GUI applications.

UI Challenge

Making console-based interactions feel modern and engaging while maintaining readability and intuitive navigation

Innovation Approach

Utilized Spectre.Console's rich formatting, progress bars, and interactive prompts to create a visually appealing terminal experience

Testing, Balancing & Refinement

The final development phase focused on extensive playtesting and iterative improvements to ensure a polished and enjoyable gaming experience:

Gameplay Testing:
  • Balance Testing: Adjusted card statistics and battle mechanics to ensure fair and challenging gameplay
  • Flow Optimization: Streamlined user interactions and reduced unnecessary complexity
User Experience Enhancement:
  • Clear Feedback: Added comprehensive prompts and status updates for all player actions
  • Visual Clarity: Implemented consistent formatting and visual separators for better navigation
  • Error Handling: Built robust input validation and user-friendly error messages
Refinement Challenge

Ensuring smooth gameplay flow and maintaining player engagement throughout extended gaming sessions in a text-based environment.

Polish Strategy

Implemented save/load functionality, added achievement tracking, and created varied gameplay scenarios to maintain long-term interest and replayability.

Outcome & Results

Completed a fully functional prototype with key gameplay systems. The project demonstrates proficiency in C# programming, object-oriented design, and creating engaging user experiences in console applications.
