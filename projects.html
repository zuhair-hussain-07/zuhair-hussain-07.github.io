Initiate

A web-based platform that connects businesses with AI-powered tools and software solutions.

Next.js Tailwind CSS Prisma ORM Clerk Auth Stripe Connect Vercel REST APIs
View Project View Code

Summary

Initiate is a web-based platform that connects businesses with AI-powered tools and software solutions. It helps users discover, compare, and adopt the right technologies for their needs — from chatbots and automation to analytics and beyond.

My Role

Served as full stack developer and built:

  • Community features with post threads, likes, and topic tagging
  • Stripe-powered payment system with invoicing and seller payouts
  • An AI-driven Resource Hub that matches businesses with grants and support schemes

Visuals

Homepage

The homepage showcases a curated list of AI solutions with filters by category, use case, and popularity. It’s designed to help users quickly discover tools that fit their business needs.

Community Page

A built-in community hub where users can ask questions, share insights, and review AI tools. This feature encourages peer-to-peer support and crowdsourced feedback on solutions.

Payment & Invoicing

Secure and seamless checkout flow powered by Stripe Connect. Users can purchase tools, receive downloadable invoices, and sellers can manage payouts from their dashboard.

Resource Hub

An AI-assisted directory of grants, government programs, and business support schemes. Companies can discover relevant opportunities, and the AI engine recommends the most suitable ones based on their business profile and data stored in the platform.

Work Process

The project began with researching how AI tools are currently discovered and bought online. After wireframing the platform experience, I built some of the core functionality — Stripe Connect onboarding, community features and resource hub.

Collaboration features like community posts and comment threads were added later to increase engagement and peer reviews.

Outcome & Results

Initiate is now a registered company, officially partnering with IMDA (Singapore) and MDEC (Malaysia) as part of their SME digitalisation agendas. With over 50 AI startups lined up to onboard their products, the platform is entering its growth phase — focused on scaling partnerships, expanding feature sets, and bringing AI adoption to more businesses across the region.

Summary

Polyguru is a productivity platform designed to empower students through Generative AI tools. It offers features like test paper generation using Gemini AI, a comprehensive GPA calculator, and a collaborative Q&A feed to enhance learning efficiency.

My Role

  • Served as a full-stack developer, working alongside a teammate.
  • Designed and implemented the Test Paper Generation tool using Gemini AI.
  • Developed the GPA Calculator and the community Q&A feed.
  • Handled database access with Prisma ORM and integrated AI components.
  • Created a responsive UI with TailwindCSS and DaisyUI.

Visuals

Test Paper Generation using Gemini AI

Developed an AI-powered tool that generates customized test papers for students based on their learning materials. Integrated with Google's Gemini AI for intelligent question creation and built a user-friendly interface for test customization.

GPA Calculator

Developed an intuitive GPA calculator tailored for polytechnic students. The tool allows users to input their grades and credit units to calculate both semester and cumulative GPAs, with an option to save and track progress over time.

Work Process

PolyGuru was born out of a shared goal between my teammates and me to improve how students prepare for exams and track their academic progress. We identified a need for tools that could automate test creation and simplify GPA management.

We worked together on the full-stack development, focusing on core features like AI-powered test paper generation and an intuitive GPA calculator, alongside a collaborative Q&A feed.

Outcome & Results

PolyGuru is now a functional platform that empowers students to create custom test papers from their notes and accurately calculate their GPA. Initial feedback has been positive, with users highlighting the test generator's effectiveness for revision and the GPA calculator's convenience.
